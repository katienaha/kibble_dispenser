ABOUT MINNIE


I was born in North Carolina in November 2019. I spent the first six months of my life wandering around, until animal control brought me to an animal shelter. On the streets, I didn't have much to eat, so I was very, very small. Luckily the shelter had plenty of food!

Right away, a very nice elderly couple saw how small and weak I was, and asked to adopt me. Little did they know, I would soon grow to be 50 pounds! Much too big and strong for them to handle.

So they asked Katie (now my mom!) and Aaron (now my dad!) if they could look after me for a while. Katie and Aaron said yes, then quickly fell in love with me - I am very cute! Now, they are my fur-ever home.
